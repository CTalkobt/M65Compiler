# cc45 Support and Contribution Model

This document outlines the tiered support and contribution model for the cc45 Mega65 C Compiler Suite. The goal is to keep the core toolchain open-source while providing a sustainable way for the author to support the community and for users to ensure the project's longevity.

## Philosophy
The cc45 suite remains open-source. We do not use "paywalls" for core functionality. Instead, we offer a "Value-for-Value" model where professional users and enthusiasts can pay for prioritized access, expertise, and peace of mind.

## Tiers

### 1. Supporter ($10)
*   **Target:** Casual hobbyists and enthusiasts.
*   **Focus:** Project sustainability and gratitude.
*   **Benefits:**
    *   Name included in the "Supporters" section of the README and documentation.
    *   The knowledge that you are helping keep the MEGA65's premier C toolchain alive.

### 2. Power User ($35)
*   **Target:** Serious enthusiasts building large non-commercial projects or complex demos.
*   **Focus:** Access and community involvement.
*   **Benefits:**
    *   All benefits of the Supporter tier.
    *   Priority on "How-To" and architectural questions.
    *   Access to early-release builds and experimental branches (e.g., v0.99.dev).

### 3. Commercial / Professional ($75)
*   **Target:** Developers releasing commercial games (digital or physical) or anyone requiring a technical safety net.
*   **Focus:** Risk mitigation and professional-grade support.
*   **Benefits:**
    *   All benefits of the Power User tier.
    *   **Priority Bug Fixing:** Critical code-generation or linker bugs reported by Pro users are moved to the top of the development queue.
    *   **Optimization Consulting:** Direct review of critical code paths with advice on leveraging 45GS02-specific optimizations (Q-registers, ZP calling conventions, etc.).
    *   **Direct Access:** A dedicated communication channel (email or Discord) for faster coordination during release cycles.

## Commercial Use Policy
While the compiler remains open-source, developers releasing commercial products are **strongly encouraged** to purchase the $75 Professional tier. This contributes to the maintenance of the tools that make their commercial success possible.
